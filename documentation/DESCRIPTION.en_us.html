Półtawski Nowy is a digital revival project of the Antykwa Półtawskiego typeface created thatnks to the support of the "Digital Culture 2020" program of the Munistry of Culture and National Heritage, and now also Google Fonts. Even though digital version of the design already existed, the authors decided to recreate the project including a broad spectrum of references: original specimen pages, technical drawings, and documentation of matrices and punches. Extended Półtawski Nowy family, including 6 instances was just updated and now includes the Google Latin Plus character set.


More info about the project available on the projects website.
